CREDIT AGREEMENT — SAMPLE CLAUSES FOR TESTING

Section 6.01. Incurrence of Indebtedness. The Borrower shall not, and shall not permit any Restricted Subsidiary to, directly or indirectly, incur any Indebtedness; provided that the Borrower may incur Indebtedness if, after giving pro forma effect thereto, the Total Leverage Ratio would not exceed 4.50 to 1.00. Additionally, Indebtedness may be incurred under a general basket not to exceed the greater of $150,000,000 and 20% of Consolidated EBITDA (the "Grower Basket"). EBITDA for purposes of this calculation shall include projected cost savings and synergies, whether or not realized, for the 24-month period following any acquisition (the "Add-Back Provision").

Section 6.04. Restricted Payments. The Borrower shall not declare or make any Restricted Payment unless: (i) no Default or Event of Default exists; and (ii) after giving pro forma effect thereto, the Total Leverage Ratio is less than 3.50 to 1.00. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Restricted Payments may be made in an aggregate amount not to exceed $75,000,000 per fiscal year pursuant to a fixed basket. Dividends to parent for general corporate purposes are permitted without limit provided no Event of Default exists.

Section 6.07. Asset Sales. The Borrower shall not, and shall not permit any of its Restricted Subsidiaries to, convey, sell, lease, transfer or otherwise dispose of any of its property or assets, except: (a) dispositions of obsolete or worn out property; (b) sales of inventory in the ordinary course of business; (c) any single transaction or series of related transactions for consideration not exceeding $50,000,000; and (d) any disposition so long as the Total Leverage Ratio, on a pro forma basis after giving effect to such disposition and any related repayment of Indebtedness, would not exceed 5.00 to 1.00. Net proceeds from asset sales exceeding $25,000,000 shall be applied to repayment of outstanding Loans within 365 days, subject to reinvestment rights.

Section 6.09. Collateral and Guarantees. The Borrower shall, and shall cause each Guarantor to, grant to the Administrative Agent a first priority perfected security interest in all assets of the Borrower and each Guarantor, including but not limited to: accounts receivable, inventory, equipment, intellectual property, and real property. No Subsidiary shall be released from its guarantee obligations without the prior written consent of the Required Lenders. Additional collateral coverage tests require maintenance of a Collateral Coverage Ratio of not less than 1.5 to 1.0 tested quarterly.

Section 9.02. Amendments and Waivers. No amendment or waiver of any provision of this Agreement shall be effective without the written consent of the Required Lenders (defined as Lenders holding more than 50% of the outstanding Commitments). Notwithstanding the foregoing, any amendment to extend the maturity of any Lender's Loans may be effected with the consent of such Lender only, without the consent of any other Lender (the "Snooze/Extend" provision). Voting rights are transferable to affiliated funds without restriction.
